# TRSM-Model – Temporal Reflected Spectrum Model. by; Bilal Muthanna

> **One Theory, One Mechanism and One Constant 4 Everything**  
> نحو إطار موحّد لفيزياء الكون وفيزياء الكم

The **Temporal Reflected Spectrum Model (TRSM)** is an ambitious research program that aims to build a unified physical picture of the universe – from quantum scales to cosmological scales – within a single coherent framework:

- **One Theory**: a single theoretical architecture that treats space, time, matter, and energy as different faces of a reflected temporal spectrum.
- **One Mechanism**: a common reflection mechanism in negative time, generating both the structure of matter (nuclear binding, particle masses, quantum levels) and the structure of the cosmos (gravity, dark matter, dark energy, and cosmic expansion).
- **One Constant**: a universal deposition scale linking nuclear binding energies, gravitational potentials, and cosmological evolution into one quantitative law.

في هذا النموذج، يتم التعامل مع فيزياء الكم والفيزياء الكونية على أنهما تجلّيان مختلفان لبنية زمنية انعكاسية واحدة، حيث يعمل الزمن السالب كخزان للمعلومة والطاقة، وتُعاد صياغة الجاذبية، والمادة المظلمة، والطاقة المظلمة، وعدم اليقين الكمومي ضمن آلية موحّدة.

## Repository Structure (planned)

- `papers/` – LaTeX manuscripts for the TRSM papers (core model, dark sector, gravity, black holes, time–uncertainty, etc.).
- `code/` – Python scripts and notebooks implementing TRSM fits (nuclear binding, galaxy rotation curves, cosmology).
- `data/` – Datasets used to test TRSM against observations.
- `docs/` – Diagrams, conceptual notes, and additional documentation.
- `arabic/` – Arabic drafts and extended explanations of the model if needed.
